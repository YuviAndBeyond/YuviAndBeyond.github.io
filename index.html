My Research Interests

My research interests lie in numerical relativity, gravitational wave detection, and the modeling of compact binary mergers. My recent work at the International Centre for Theoretical Sciences (ICTS-TIFR), Bangalore focuses on the low-latency detection of eccentric binary black hole mergers using machine learning techniques. This project aims to advance the capabilities of LIGO's upcoming observational runs by detecting gravitational wave signals with varying degrees of orbital eccentricity.
